Soybean and Corn Advisor, Inc

The mission of Soybean And Corn Advisor is to provide our readers with the most up-to-date information about soybean and corn production that you can't find anywhere else. On this site, you will find articles that deal primarily with soybean and corn production in South America, but we will also inform our readers about other crops such as wheat, cotton, and sugarcane in addition to alcohol and biodiesel production. The Soybean and Corn Advisor does not trade the commodity markets or give trading advice and we never have a position in the markets. This allows us to supply accurate information to our readers regardless of market activity.

The Soybean And Corn Advisor offers a variety of services including: Website, Newsletter, Premier Service, and Presentations.

Dr. Michael Cordonnier Biography

Dr. Cordonnier holds a Ph.D. in agronomy from Michigan State University and has been involved in global crop production for more than 35 years. His experience in South America started in the early 1970's when he conducted agricultural experiments in central Brazil. He has been a keen observer of the development of the soybean industry in South America over the last thirty years. Dr. Cordonnier is fluent in Portuguese and travels regularly to Brazil, Argentina, and Paraguay to inspect the soybean and corn crops. He appears frequently on national television and radio programs to discuss world agricultural issues and he conducts numerous seminars dealing with agricultural production for regional and national audiences. He is now president of Soybean And Corn Advisor, Inc., a consulting firm that works with many Fortune 500 companies and the agricultural trading community specializing in fundamental analysis of soybean and corn production with special emphasis on South America.

Web Site

In addition to providing up-to-date agricultural information such as crop acreages and crop production, the Soybean And Corn Advisor web site also provides information concerning South American cropping cycles, weather, infrastructure, land utilization, ethanol production, and alternative fuels. Newsletter

The Soybean And Corn Advisor also publishes a weekly Newsletter that provides up-to-date analysis of soybean and corn production. In addition to weekly production estimates, the Newsletter also examines crop conditions, crop progress, weather patterns, governmental policies, rural economic conditions and market forces that shape and effect what's happening in the field.
